Michigan Bar Prep Essays by year

So here's all the questions and answers (starting in 2015, go back to 2009 next time I sit down to work on this) organized by topic. As I study, I'll add more resources. I might also pull together outlines of each topic that just consist of the law inferred from the test answers. If anyone using this resource has any links you want to share, or if you've manually copied out a question or response already, or you have the topic coding for an earlier year, email me at e.feasley@gmail.com.

The Michigan website has every exam is available from 2009 - 2020, and my plan is to link to the essays in reverse chronological order.
